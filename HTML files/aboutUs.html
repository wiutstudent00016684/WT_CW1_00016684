Why Us?

Learn without leaving home Innovative technologies transform the educational process for teachers and students worldwide. In accordance with the 2030 Concept for the Development of the Education System of the Republic of Uzbekistan, active measures are being taken to modernize the system.
The concept outlines tasks such as applying modern information and communication technologies and innovative projects to the education system, implementing contemporary methods and directions for teaching and upbringing students beyond school, as well as providing them with opportunities for development. The Innovation, Technology, and Strategy Center (ITSM) sets goals for implementing these programs.
